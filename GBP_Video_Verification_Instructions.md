# How to Record Your Google Business Profile Verification Video

## Before You Start

- You **cannot** pre-record this video and upload it later. You must record it live through the Google Business Profile app on your phone.
- The video must be **one continuous shot** — no cuts, no editing.
- It needs to be **at least 30 seconds long** (aim for about 60-90 seconds to cover everything).
- Plan what you're going to show **before** you hit record.

---

## What to Film (3 Things, In This Order)

### 1. Your Neighborhood / Location (~15 seconds)
Walk outside and slowly film:
- A **street sign** near your home/base
- Your **street number** on your house or mailbox
- Any recognizable **landmarks** nearby (a park, a business, an intersection)

This proves your business operates from the address you listed.

### 2. Your Truck and Equipment (~30 seconds)
Film your **flatbed tow truck** — this is the most important part:
- Walk around the truck slowly so Google can see the whole thing
- If you have any **business name, logo, or phone number** on the truck, make sure the camera clearly shows it
- Show your **towing equipment** — straps, winch, controls, etc.
- If you have **business cards**, hold one up to the camera

### 3. Proof You're the Owner (~15 seconds)
Show that you actually manage this business:
- **Unlock the truck** on camera (shows only you have access)
- **Get in the cab** and show the dashboard/controls
- If you have any **business documents** — a business license, a recent invoice, a utility bill with "Tavio Transportation" on it — hold it up to the camera briefly

---

## Do NOT Show
- Your face or anyone else's face (Google says to avoid other people's faces)
- Bank account numbers, tax ID, Social Security number
- Any sensitive personal documents

---

## How to Submit

1. Open the **Google Business Profile** on your phone
2. Tap **"Get Verified"**
3. Enter your phone number if asked, tap Next
4. Tap **"Business video"** then **Next**
5. Allow access to your camera, microphone, and location when prompted
6. Tap **Next**, then **Start Recording**
7. Film everything listed above in one smooth take
8. Review it — if you missed something, tap **"Try again"**
9. When it looks good, tap **"Upload video"**

---

## After You Submit

- Google will review the video — this takes **up to 5 business days**
- If approved, you'll get a notification that your business is verified
- If rejected, Google will tell you exactly what was missing (e.g., "business name not visible" or "no nearby area shown") — you can re-record and try again

---

## Quick Checklist Before Hitting Record

- [ ] I'm at my home/business address
- [ ] I can see a street sign or landmark from here
- [ ] My truck is parked and accessible
- [ ] I have my keys to unlock the truck on camera
- [ ] I have a business document with "Tavio Transportation" on it (invoice, license, or business card)
- [ ] My phone has enough storage and battery
- [ ] I'm logged into the Google account that manages the Business Profile
